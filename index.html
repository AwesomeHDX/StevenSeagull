Steven Seagull

A game created in just 36 hours at the Arctic Game Jam, Tromsø, 12-14 September 2014

Tromsø is being terrorized by a cursed were-seagull. In order to turn back into a man, the seagull must pass the curse on to another by connecting with an attack.

Steven is walking along the sea-front eating a tasty kebab. He must try to finish his meal while avoiding the seagull's attacks.

How to play

  1. Player one starts as Seagull; player 2 starts as Steven.
  2. Both players move at a fixed speed and can change direction by pressing button 1.
  3. Seagull can attack Steven by pressing button 2.
  4. Steven can eat his kebab by pressing button 2. While he is eating, his "shish-ke-bar" reduces slowly, and he is more vulnerable to attack.
  5. If Seagull’s attack connects, the curse is passed on and the players change places, with Steven becoming Seagull and vice versa.
  6. The first player to finish their kebab (as Steven) wins the game.
